'Last Men Standing'

In March, The Chronicle published “Last Men Standing,” an in-depth look at the issues facing longtime survivors of HIV and AIDS through the lives of eight Bay Area men. It was a yearlong reporting effort that also produced a feature-length documentary that has been screened at 10 film festivals across the nation and internationally.

Here, we present an update on those men, on the impacts of the “Last Men Standing” project, and on what has changed – and what hasn’t – in public awareness and the response to the plight of those aging with HIV and AIDS. Read about the men's journeys since the project's debut, and revisit the original project.
